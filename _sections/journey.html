Journey to Recognition is a 360° video-based virtual reality game aimed at helping adults with developmental disabilities in recognizing emotions in realistic scenarios by using emotional cues and contextual clues. By incorporating 360° video into a virtual reality setting, users are able to view and learn from realistic emotional cues portrayed from our actors.

Users are able to practice in a variety of settings, including studying at the library with friends, having lunch with friends and a job interview. Allowing users to practice identifying emotional cues and contextual clues in these settings will assist users with applying learned skills in the real world whenever they encounter similar situations. Journey to Recognition was developed using the Go Pro Fusion, DaVinci Resolve 15 and Unity for the Oculus Go.
