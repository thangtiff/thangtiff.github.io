In collaboration with global cleft organization, Smile Train, I am leading the design and development of a serious VR game aimed at providing psychosocial support to preteens with cleft lip and/or palate (CLP). I've worked with Smile Train's partnering psychologists and preteens with CLP in North and South America to identify the psychosocial needs of preteens with CLP. Together we've created a two-player game that allows the psychologist and their patient to come together to develop the patient's self-esteem and self-confidence through completing quests with the help of their SuperPet Pal. User testing is currently underway and the game will be completed in Fall 2022.
