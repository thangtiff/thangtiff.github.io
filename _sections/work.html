VR for the Psychosocial Support of Preteens with CLP

I am currently working with global cleft organization, Smile Train, in developing a VR game aimed at providing psychosocial support to preteens with Cleft Lip and/or Palate (CLP). The game focuses on aiding preteens with CLP in building and developing self-confidence and will be used as a resource in addition to existing support from psychologists and therapists. The game is a collaborative effort between myself, Smile Train stakeholders and partnering psychologists around the globe to increase access to essential psychosocial support for preteens with CLP.
