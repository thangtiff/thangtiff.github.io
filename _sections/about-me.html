Over the past 6 years, I’ve conducted UX Research, and designed and developed serious VR games with UC Santa Cruz’s ASSIST Lab as a Graduate Student Researcher. My work focuses on promoting the psychological well-being of individuals with disabilities and special needs through the design, development, and evaluation of VR technologies in collaboration with non-profit organizations.

My research interests include:

  • User Experience Design and Research
  • Human Computer Interaction
  • Virtual Reality (VR)
  • Assistive Technologies
  • Serious Games
  • Usability/Accessability

To learn more about my work, check out my portfolio.
