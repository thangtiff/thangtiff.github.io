I am a Computational Media Ph.D Candidate in the ASSIST Lab at the University of California, Santa Cruz. I lead the design, development and evaluation of serious virtual reality (VR) games for organizations providing support to people with special needs.

My research interests include:

  • Serious Games
  • Human Computer Interaction
  • Virtual Reality (VR)
  • Assistive Technologies
  • Usability/Accessability
  • User Experience Design and Research
