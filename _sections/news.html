July 29th, 2022:

NSF SBIR Seed Funding awarded to VRIENDS and UCSC ASSIST Lab collaborators, Tiffany Thang, Aviv Elor and Sri Kurniawan, for research and development of a VR environment helping children with autism spectrum disorders in learning and practicing social skills.

May 10, 2021:

Smile Train research grant awarded to Tiffany Thang and Sri Kurniawan for the development of a serious VR game to help preteens with cleft lip and/or palate with developing psychosocial skills in the realm of self-esteem and self-confidence.

December 14, 2021:

NSF IIS: Human-Centered Computing (HCC) grant awared to Tiffany Thang and Sri Kurniawan for the research and development of a VR game focused on helping adults with developmental disabilities in learning and strengthening social-emotional and independent living skills.
