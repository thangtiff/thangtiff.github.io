EmotionVR was created to assist adults with developmental disabilities in understanding how to identify emotions through facial expressions. Users were taught to use information from a character's facial features collectively to recognize what emotion they were portraying. This game was created for the HTC Vive using Unity, Blender and Fuse.

I presented my work on EmotionVR at UC Santa Cruz's 2018 Grad Slam.
