EmotionVR was a detective-themed VR game created to assist adults with developmental disabilities in understanding how to identify emotions through identifying facial expressions collectively. I presented my work on EmotionVR at the UC Santa Cruz 2018 Grad Slam competition, where I discussed the game and its impact in a 3-minute presentation competition. I have also presented and published my work at the International Conference on Disability, Virtual Reality & Associated Technologies in 2018 (ICDVRAT 2018) and the International Conference on Smart Computing (SMARTCOMP 2018).
